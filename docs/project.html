How to use Budgie?

Budgie is an open-source music programming kit. You can get the Budgie Kit in two ways:

1. Prepare the 3D blocks with a material of your choice, and use NFC stickers to define the models. You can access the model files, code and guidelines from our Github page

2. Buy a Budgie Kit.

2

Curriculum and Tutorials

We prepared a curriculum to introduce technology to young minds and develop their computational thinking skills.

The curriculum is intended to 9-12 age group, educators can adapt the curriculum for lower and upper age groups.

3

Research and Development

Budgie is a tangible and low-cost programming kit for an inclusive programming education.

The kit includes 24 pieces of DIY blocks with pre-scanned NFC stickers to recognize the blocks.
